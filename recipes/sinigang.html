How to cook Sinigang

Sinigang in the Philippines

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines.

This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
  • 1 piece onion sliced
  • 2 tomatoes quartered
  • 1 bunch of pechay
  • 2 pieces of taro quartered
  • 2 quarts water
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a cooking pot
  2. Add the pork belly, onions, and tomatoes
  3. Add sinigang mix once the water boils again
  4. Add the taro. Cook until the taro can be mashed
  5. Once the pork belly is soft, add salt to taste
  6. Add the pechay. Cook for 3 minutes
  7. Serve hot with rice
