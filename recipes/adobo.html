How to cook Adobo

Adobo in the Philippines

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo.

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 piece onion sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 5 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 5 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 pieces of bay leaves
  • peppercorn as desired

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, soy sauce, and vinegar. Marinade for 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes
  3. Pour the remaining marinade
  4. Add water, whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  5. Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Serve hot with rice
