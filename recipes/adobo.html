How to cook Adobo

Adobo in the Philippines

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo.

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 onionsliced
  • 1 piece onionsliced
  • 2 tomatoesquartered
  • 1 bunch of pechay
  • 2 pieces of taroquartered
  • 2 quarts water
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a cooking pot
  2. Add the pork belly, onions, and tomatoes
  3. Add sinigang mix once the water boils again
  4. Add the taro. Cook until the taro can be mashed
  5. Once the pork belly is soft, add salt to taste
  6. Add the pechay. Cook for 3 minutes
  7. Serve hot with rice
